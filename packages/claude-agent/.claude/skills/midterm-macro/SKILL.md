# Midterm Macro Options Playbook

Broader options plays across 5 sectors with a 30-90 day horizon. These positions capture macro catalysts while hedging against portfolio concentration in AI supply chain names.

## Energy Sector

Thesis: Geopolitical instability (Strait of Hormuz, OPEC+ discipline, Russia/Ukraine) creates persistent upside risk in oil. Energy is a natural hedge against AI infrastructure costs.

| Ticker | Role | Catalyst | Options Play |
|--------|------|----------|-------------|
| XLE | Energy sector ETF | Broad sector exposure | Buy calls on geopolitical escalation, sell puts on dips |
| XOM | Integrated oil major | Strait of Hormuz beneficiary, Guyana ramp | Sell cash-secured puts at support, buy calls on breakouts |
| CVX | Integrated oil major | Permian Basin, LNG export growth | Pair trade on relative value vs XOM |

## Cross-Sector Hedging Logic

| Scenario | Winners | Losers |
|----------|---------|--------|
| AI capex boom continues | Supply chain, AI semis | Hedges expire worthless |
| Geopolitical escalation | Energy, Defense | Broad market dips |
| Recession / rate spike | Defense, Hedges pay | AI supply chain, AI semis |

When making recommendations: check current portfolio allocation, recommend hedges if overweight one scenario, size to maintain balance, never let any single sector exceed 50% of buying power.
